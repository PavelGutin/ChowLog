Introduction

Welcome to ChowLog ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our application and tell you about your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

Camera Data

  • What we collect: When you use our app's camera functionality, we temporarily access your device's camera to capture images of food.
  • How we use it: These images are stored locally on your device to help you maintain a log of your meals.
  • Data storage: All images are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Storage Access

  • What we access: Our app requires access to your device's storage to save and retrieve images.
  • How we use it: We only use this permission to store and access the food images you capture within the app.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for their commercial purposes.

Data Security
